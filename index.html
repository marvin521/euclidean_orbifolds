Orbifold Tutte Embeddings

Noam Aigerman & Yaron Lipman

Matlab code implementing the Siggraph Asia 2015 paper, "Orbifold Tutte Embeddings". An extension of Tutte's embedding for spheres and other topologies: a mesh is cut open, and embedded with appropriate boundary conditions so that the resulting embedding can tile the plane and yield a tiling which is discrete-harmonic everywhere. The embedding is thus guaranteed to be bijective, and also seamless in the sense that modifying the cuts yields the same tiling.
